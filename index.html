What are the required inputs for a Management Review under ISO 9001 Clause 9.3?
N
ISO 9001:2015 Clause 9.3.2 requires 8 mandatory inputs:

1. Status of actions from previous reviews
2. Changes in external/internal issues (4.1)
3. Customer satisfaction & stakeholder feedback
4. Quality objectives achievement
5. Process performance & product conformity
6. Nonconformities & corrective actions
7. Monitoring & measurement results
8. Audit results & external provider performance

⚠ Major NC if any input is missing.
✓ Best practice Document each with evidence.
